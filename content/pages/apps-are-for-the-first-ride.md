Rideshare apps can introduce you to passengers. SoloDrive helps you keep the relationship after trust is built.

## The opportunity

A rider gets in your car, enjoys the ride, and asks whether you will be around later. That is the moment most drivers lose because they do not have infrastructure.

## The SoloDrive answer

SoloDrive gives drivers a booking page, intake flow, trip status surface, and payment path so passengers can book directly next time.
