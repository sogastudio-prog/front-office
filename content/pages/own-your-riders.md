Drivers do not need more random rides. They need a way to turn the trust they already build in the car into repeat direct bookings.

## The problem

Uber and Lyft own the customer relationship. Drivers do the service work, but the rider relationship stays inside the app.

## The SoloDrive viewpoint

Apps are for the first ride. Your business is built on the second.

## What changes

- You get a direct booking page.
- Riders can request you again.
- Your booking link becomes part of your business.
- Every completed ride can become a future booking opportunity.
