<section class="sd-section sd-hero sd-hero--trust">
  <p class="sd-eyebrow">Privacy</p>

  <h1>Privacy, stated plainly.</h1>

  <p class="sd-hero__lead">SoloDrive uses information to support website access, onboarding, booking workflows, communication, payment-related infrastructure, and operational records.</p>
</section>

<section class="sd-section sd-card">
  <p class="sd-eyebrow">Information submitted through the site</p>

  <h2>When you submit information, it may be used to respond, onboard, or support your account.</h2>

  <p>This may include information submitted through forms, request flows, onboarding steps, booking workflows, support messages, or other direct interactions with SoloDrive.</p>

  <p>Examples may include name, email, phone number, business details, service area, submitted ride or booking details, and related operational information.</p>
</section>

<section class="sd-section sd-card">
  <p class="sd-eyebrow">Third-party services</p>

  <h2>Some platform functions rely on outside providers.</h2>

  <p>SoloDrive may rely on third-party providers for hosting, analytics, communication, payment-related infrastructure, maps, security, and operational services.</p>

  <p>When those services are used, relevant information may be processed according to the provider’s own terms and privacy practices.</p>
</section>

<section class="sd-section sd-card">
  <p class="sd-eyebrow">Payments and transactions</p>

  <h2>Payment-related information may be handled by payment infrastructure providers.</h2>

  <p>Where payment functionality is enabled, payment details, transaction records, disputes, receipts, and related workflow data may be handled through third-party payment processors.</p>

  <p>SoloDrive may store related operational records needed to support transaction workflows, receipts, platform fees, dispute documentation, and account support.</p>
</section>

<section class="sd-section sd-card">
  <p class="sd-eyebrow">Operational records</p>

  <h2>Booking, trip, and workflow records may be retained for operational reasons.</h2>

  <p>Records may be used to support account setup, trip coordination, booking history, receipts, support requests, payment workflows, audit trails, and platform reliability.</p>

  <p>These records help make direct rides more structured, accountable, and repeatable.</p>
</section>

<section class="sd-section sd-card">
  <p class="sd-eyebrow">Retention and security</p>

  <h2>We retain information for operational, legal, and security reasons as needed.</h2>

  <p>SoloDrive takes reasonable measures to protect information handled through the platform, but no website or software system can guarantee absolute security.</p>

  <p>Information may be retained as long as reasonably necessary to support operations, maintain records, resolve disputes, comply with legal obligations, or protect the platform.</p>
</section>

<section class="sd-section sd-card">
  <p class="sd-eyebrow">Your choices</p>

  <h2>If you have questions about information submitted through the site, contact us directly.</h2>

  <p>You may request clarification regarding information you submitted through forms, onboarding interactions, booking workflows, or support communications.</p>

  <p>Use the contact path made available on the site for privacy-related questions.</p>
</section>
