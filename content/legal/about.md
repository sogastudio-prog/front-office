<section class="sd-section sd-card">
  <p class="sd-eyebrow">What SoloDrive is not</p>

  <h2>It is not a transportation operator. It is not a marketplace trying to own the rider.</h2>

  <p>SoloDrive is not trying to become another company that sits between the driver and the relationship forever.</p>

  <p>The goal is to provide the infrastructure layer that helps drivers keep more of what they already earn.</p>
