<section class="sd-section sd-card sd-solution-flow">
  <p class="sd-eyebrow">How it works</p>

  <h2>How the SoloDrive ride flow works.</h2>

  <ol>
    <li><strong>Rider opens your booking page.</strong> You share your booking link with riders who want to book you directly.</li>
    <li><strong>Rider requests a ride.</strong> The rider enters pickup, dropoff, timing, and contact details.</li>
    <li><strong>Ride moves into your operator workflow.</strong> You review the request, manage the quote and payment path, and prepare the ride.</li>
    <li><strong>Rider uses the trip page.</strong> The rider receives a private trip page for status and coordination.</li>
    <li><strong>Ride is completed.</strong> The trip becomes part of your ride history and business records.</li>
    <li><strong>Rider can book you again.</strong> Every completed ride becomes another chance to build a repeat customer.</li>
  </ol>
</section>
